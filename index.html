Protecting Our Oceans Together

Why Oceans Matter

Oceans cover 71% of our planet and are home to amazing creatures. They give us oxygen, food, and help regulate our climate.

Ocean Problems

Our oceans face many threats:

  • Plastic pollution
  • Overfishing
  • Climate change
  • Coral reef damage

How You Can Help

  • Use less plastic (bring reusable bags and bottles)
  • Pick up trash when you visit the beach
  • Learn about ocean animals
  • Tell others why oceans are important
