New Glowing Species Found in Deep Sea

June 15, 2023

Scientists have discovered three new bioluminescent species during an expedition to the Mariana Trench. The fish emit unique light patterns never seen before.

June 10, 2023

Northern sections of the Great Barrier Reef show the highest coral coverage in decades, according to new surveys. Experts credit recent conservation efforts.

Whale Communication Study Reveals Complex Language

June 5, 2023

A new MIT study suggests humpback whales may use syntax in their songs, showing language complexity similar to humans. Researchers recorded over 1,000 hours of whale sounds.
